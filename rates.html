How Estimates Are Calculated

Step 1Count room points (each room type is worth a set number of points)
Step 2Multiply total points × base rate to get the raw base price
Step 3Apply percentage adjustments (scope + occupancy + condition)
Step 4Apply deep clean multiplier (first-time and one-time cleans only)
Step 5Apply recurring service discount (weekly, biweekly, or monthly)
Step 6Add flat fees: pet surcharge and any selected add-ons
